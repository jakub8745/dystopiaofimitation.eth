I began my practice of sculpting through digital realms with the project 'Sculpting in 3D' [ Art Council England Grant ], which culminated in the exhibition 'The Dystopia of an imitation' at Blue Point Art Gallery [2022] and a book of the same title.

'Dystopia of Imitation' is an evolving digital art installation. It arises from a fascination and exploration of digital sculptural techniques and materials. Doi is a practice of sculpting through various meta-spaces and artistic ideas using 3D modelling, 3D scanning, programming techniques.
